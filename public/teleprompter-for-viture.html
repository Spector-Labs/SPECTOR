FAQ

Viture teleprompter questions.

Does Viture have a built-in teleprompter?

No. Viture's glasses and SpaceWalker app give you a virtual display for your phone or PC, but there's no first-party teleprompter. To read a script eyes-up you run a teleprompter on that screen — which is what Spector is built for.

Which Viture models work?

Any Viture that mirrors your phone or computer — Luma, Luma Pro, and Beast — since Spector runs in the browser on that display. You can also rehearse entirely on your phone first.

Do I have to install anything?

No app store needed. Spector is a PWA — open it in a browser, and optionally install it for offline use. The core is free and open source.

Is it really free?

Yes — the core teleprompter is free forever, no watermark, no account. Try it right now.
